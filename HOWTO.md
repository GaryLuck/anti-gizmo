 ### How it Works

  1. Interactive Controls: Built for iPad touch screens. The robot smoothly rolls left and right, following the
  user's touch or drag coordinates.
  2. Collectibles: Gears (worth 1 point), Batteries (worth 2 points + charges magnet power), and glowing Microchips
  (worth 5 points + twinkly chime).
  3. No Game Over: If the robot hits falling scrap metal (tires, heavy beams, old gears), it spins in a dizzy loop,
  plays a goofy sound, and drops a few of its collected gears on the floor. Your grandson can quickly roll over them
  to collect them again!
  4. Turbo Magnet Power: Collecting batteries fills the Magnet Power Bar. Once it hits 100%, the robot turns into a
  magnet with a rainbow forcefield, pulling all items on screen directly to itself for 5 seconds.
  5. Milestone Celebrations: Every 50 points, a celebratory "Level Up" badge pops up with falling colored confetti to
  congratulate him.
  6. Customizable Paint: He can tap and customize Robo's neon paint color (Cyan, Pink, Green, Gold) on the main menu.
  7. Synthesized Audio: All sounds are synthesized in real time using the browser's Web Audio API (no external file
  dependencies required).
